Surfing

Welcome to my surfing page! Here's a list of places I like to surf and why I like them:

  1. Strands
      2. It is a fast break with a nice left
      It is also can be very hollow

  2. T-Street
      3. I grew up going here and it has a lot of nostalgia
      The break is also fun with the option of a right or a left and can be super glassy

  3. Las Gaviotas
      4. The waves can stack for super nice long sets
      It also isn't too fast or too slow

  4. Salt Creek
      5. It has a heavy wave that is super fun when it gets big
      The point is super fun to surf with a nice left

  5. Sano
      6. Slow rolling waves that give a long ride
      It is fun with a long board
